Biography

I am a fourth-year at the University of Texas at Austin. I am a biology major and hope to go to medical school. I serve in the Kowalski Lab at UT Health Austin, where I conduct molecular subtype analyses on genetic and genomic cancer datasets. I also work in the Freshman Research Initiative stream, Microbe Hackers, where I help freshmen learn the ins and outs of scientific research. I code quite a bit for fun and am really into generative art, neural networks, and software engineering. I have also started my journey as a budding social entrepreneur, thanks to the UT student organization, Social Entrepreneurship Learning Lab.
